WHO CAN ENTER?:

The Contest is open to U.S. residents ONLY. Must be 18 years and older to enter, residents of AL and NE must be 19 years and older.

A First and Second Prize Winner will be selected each week beginning the week of June 17, 2016 through July 24, 2016. All winners will be contacted by a Dollamur Customer Representative.

WHAT YOU CAN WIN:

First Prizes (5 Available) - One 10x10 FLEXI-Connect® Stunt Pad® (value $449). Winner is drawn weekly.
