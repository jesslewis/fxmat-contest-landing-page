The Grand Prize Winner will be selected from among all of the First and Second Prize weekly winners based on the judging criteria outlined above. The Post that receives the highest total score from the judges from among all First and Second Prize winners will be declared the potential Grand Prize Winner. Grand Prize judging will take place on or about June 2, 2016.
